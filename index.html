Been developing driver, and its accompanying desktop GUI application for one of the leading companies in its field for a number of years now. Now I mainly develop for Linux, and Chrome OS, but also do consulting for Windows(as MiniDriver and UniDrv), and Mac OS due to my years of experience in those domains.

I enjoy solving the challenges of programming most especially in logic sequencing, web developing, and optimizing codes. My main technical skills are modern C/C++ with Qt framework, Python, Bash, and Adobe PostScript. Though that is what my job requires me to master, I also have practical knowledge in Java, and some web technologies: HTML, CSS, Javascript, MERN stack, common frameworks such as Bootstrap and jQuery, and others more. Whenever there is a new technology necessary for my career or its growth, I am eager to self-study about it.

Aside from end-to-end development, I also manage and lead: projects, members, and components. Doing everything necessary in between. With a forward thinking approach, I consistently deliver and oftentimes even out deliver what is expected from me. Repeatedly solving problems in the processes, structures, information sharing among other things. What satisfies me the most however is to have a positive impact on the lives of my members by mentoring them to bring out their professional best.

I know I will be in this industry for a while, and I want to spend it subjectively well. By that I meant: mentor more members, be a part in a society-beneficial software solutions, endless learning, and craft mastery.
