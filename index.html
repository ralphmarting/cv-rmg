about
—

Started my career as a Software Engineer - immersed into the full development life cycle of desktop driver softwares for different major operating systems for more than half a decade. Including leading key software releases, and even leading a few components organizing members' effort since my mid-senior role. Guiding them so they don’t repeat the same mistakes I did. Challenging myself to bigger, and bigger challenges aligned to my personal goals, and beliefs which are compounded from the years of consistently, and consciously learning.

Since being given the role of Deputy Section Manager (a front-line leader) barely a few years ago, my primary focus has shifted into developing developers, emphasizing on a human-first approach. With size of almost 20 at its peak, and even at a point where more than half are fresh from their careers. The rewarding part is seeing each grow with every mentorship, and exposure to challenges I shared with each one. I subject myself to my members' scrutiny periodically to gather insights from another perspective to further my efficiency and effectiveness. One of the main feedback from my members and somewhat I consider my trademark is my ability to create a workspace where work-life is as frictionless as my influence can provide. A healthy team culture which I can proudly say I worked hard for through consistent efforts.

Throughout my career spanning almost a decade so far, I've learned that my key characteristics to name a few are: high-adaptability, reexamining norms, being human in a seemingly binary-focused industry, constant self-improvement - all consequently contributing to finding myself complying to the campground rule of: leaving the place better than I found it.
